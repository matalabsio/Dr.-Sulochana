## 6. Reviews (ops — steady drip)

| Do | Don’t |
|----|--------|
| Aim for **3–5 genuine Google reviews / week** | Don’t dump 50 reviews in 2 days |
| Ask patients to name the treatment (“IVF”, “PCOS consultation”, “high-risk pregnancy”) | Don’t script fake reviews or offer cash-for-reviews |
| Reply to **every** review in **24–48 hours** | Don’t leave 1-star reviews unanswered |
| Thank + invite offline follow-up for complaints | Don’t argue publicly |
